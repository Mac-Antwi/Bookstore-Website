About Us

Mission Statement

Garden City is a bookstore that seeks to provide a diverse range of books across various genres to students, researchers and avid readers. The bookstore also provides online services such as purchasing and access to bibliographies of available books. This project aims to design a user-friendly interface and experience for customers to easily browse, purchase and enjoy their favourite reads from the comfort of their homes.
